Param Pujya respected Swami Sureshanand Ji Teerth got the first vaccine of covid-vaccine, along with Mataji Archanaji Bharadwaj Mataji Shivani Teerth, administered the first vaccine of covid-vaccine at Prime Hospital Dewas today. Revered Guru ji's message to the national Revered "Gurudev ji" is requested that all of you should get this vaccine without any hesitation.
